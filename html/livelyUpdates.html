Your Questions Answered!

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Our standard check-in time is 3:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Do you offer parking facilities?

Yes, we provide complimentary parking for guests during their stay. Please inquire about availability upon arrival.

Are pets allowed at the hotel?

Unfortunately, we do not allow pets at Urban Oasis Hotel, with the exception of service animals.

Do you have smoking rooms?

No, Urban Oasis Hotel is a smoke-free property. Smoking is strictly prohibited in all indoor areas.

Is there Wi-Fi available?

Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel for all guests.

Do you have a gym or fitness center?

Yes, we have a fully equipped fitness center available for guests to use during their stay.
